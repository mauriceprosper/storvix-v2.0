FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the 14-day free trial work?+
Sign up, pick any plan, get full access for 14 days — no credit card needed. After trial, you'll be prompted to subscribe to keep your store live. Pay nothing during trial. No automatic billing.
What's the commission on sales?+
Zero. We take 0% commission. You pay a fixed monthly subscription, and your buyers pay a small ₦100 processing fee per order. The rest goes straight to your wallet.
How fast are payouts?+
Lite: 24 hours. Basic: priority. Plus and Pro: same-day. Withdrawals are powered by the Paystack Transfer API and arrive in your bank account directly.
Do you handle delivery?+
We show buyers live delivery rates from GIG, Kwik, and Sendbox at checkout via Shipbubble. The delivery fee is credited to your wallet so you can arrange logistics yourself or use a Shipbubble courier directly.
Can I use my own domain?+
Custom domains are coming soon. For now, every store gets a clean URL like your-store.storvix.ng — fully branded, SSL-secured, and fast.
What about WhatsApp notifications?+
Every order triggers automatic WhatsApp messages: confirmation to buyer, alert to seller. Shipping updates, delivery confirmations, abandoned cart recovery (Plus+), review requests — all automatic.
Is this for marketplaces?+
No. Storvix is for individual brand owners. Each seller gets their own independent store. There's no combined marketplace feed and no Storvix branding to compete with yours.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard anytime. Your store remains live until the end of your current billing period. No fees, no friction.
Do you support food vendors?+
Not yet. Storvix is currently built for physical products like fashion, beauty, electronics, and home decor. Food and time-sensitive perishables aren't a fit until we add real-time delivery dispatch.
