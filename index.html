FAQ

Questions, answered

Is the ₦1,000 a subscription or one-time?

One-time. You pay ₦1,000 to start, get full Pro features for 30 days, then choose a monthly plan from ₦2,100/month if you want to continue. Or stop. Your call.

How does the money reach me?

Buyers pay via Paystack. Money settles in your Storvix wallet within seconds. You withdraw to your Nigerian bank account whenever you want — payouts processed within 24 hours.

What does Storvix charge per transaction?

₦100 service fee per buyer order — paid by the buyer at checkout, not deducted from you. Your full price plus delivery lands in your wallet. Paystack's standard processing fee applies separately.

Do I need a CAC or RC number?

Not to start. You can sell as a sole trader. For a verified business badge, you'll need CAC documents — but most sellers ship orders for months before doing that.

Can I move my Instagram store here?

Absolutely. Most of our sellers came from IG and WhatsApp. Storvix doesn't replace your social — it captures the orders that would otherwise be lost in DMs.

Is my customer data private?

Yes. Storvix doesn't share or sell seller data. Your customer list is yours. Export it whenever you want.
