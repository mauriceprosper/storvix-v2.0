FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Sign up, pick any plan, and get full access for 14 days — no credit card required. After 14 days, you'll be prompted to subscribe to keep your store live.

What's the commission on sales?

Zero. We take 0% commission on your sales. You pay a fixed monthly subscription, and your buyers pay a small ₦100 processing fee per order. The rest goes to your wallet.

How fast are payouts?

Lite plan: 24 hours. Basic: priority. Plus and Pro: same-day. Withdrawals are powered by the Paystack Transfer API and arrive in your bank account directly.

Do you handle delivery?

We show buyers live delivery rates from GIG, Kwik, and Sendbox via Shipbubble at checkout. The delivery fee is credited to your wallet so you can arrange your own logistics.

Can I use my own domain?

Custom domains are coming soon. For now, every store gets a clean URL like your-store.storvix.ng.

What about WhatsApp notifications?

Every order triggers automatic WhatsApp messages: confirmation to buyer, alert to you. Shipping updates, delivery confirmations, and abandoned cart recovery (Plus+) all happen automatically.

Is this for marketplaces?

No. Storvix is for individual brand owners. Each seller gets their own independent store. There's no combined marketplace feed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your dashboard anytime. Your store remains live until the end of your current billing period.

Do you support food vendors?

Not in the current version — delivery speed cannot be guaranteed. Storvix is built for physical products like fashion, beauty, and electronics.
