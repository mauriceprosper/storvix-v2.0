When do I actually earn the ₦200?

When the seller you referred signs up AND pays the ₦1,000 starter pack. Just clicking the link doesn't trigger payment — they have to complete onboarding.

Once your balance hits ₦3,000. We pay directly to your Nigerian bank account, processed within 24 hours of you requesting withdrawal.

Is there a cap?

No. Refer 5 people or 5,000 — every paid signup earns you ₦200.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals are blocked. The system checks the referrer's account against the new signup's account.

No. The affiliate program is open to anyone — students, freelancers, anyone with a network. If you ARE already a Storvix seller, your dashboard already has a Refer & Earn tab — use that instead.
